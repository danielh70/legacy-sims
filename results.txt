in the "slot1" upgrade slot you can either put one of two upgrades, faster reload 4 which gives +5% acc and +5% damage or enhanced scope 4, which gives 10% acc, and in slot 2 upgrade slot you can put faster ammo 4 which gives +20% damage, or tracer rounds 4 which gives +15% acc and +5% damage.

for the void bow you can only add 1 upgrade slot, either laser sight or poisoned tip. poisoned tip increases damage by 10% and laser sight increases accuracy by 14%.
